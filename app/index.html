Intro

I would love to help your with your website, feel free to get in touch!

My skills are broad: from frontend and backend development to UX and graphic design, I enjoy each aspect, and love building sites & mobile apps from start to finish, for clients all over the world.

I value simple content structure, clean design patterns, and thoughtful interactions. My approach to website design is to create a website that strengthens your company’s brand while ensuring ease of use and simplicity for your audience.

I enjoy bringing ideas to life in the browser and combining design and business logic to achieve a user-facing product. To do this successfully, a wide skill set is necessary to produce a quality user experience that leads to meeting business goals.

While most of my attention is on interactive projects, I do branding and collateral from time to time. This includes logo design, business cards, and other printed materials

About

I’m a frontend developer and graphic designer living in Prague with experience in many different areas of web development from frontend engineering to user experience and visual design.

I have worked on several web and print based projects for a range of clients providing design services (Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, XD) and web development as well (HTML, CSS, SASS, JavaScript, React, PHP, WordPress, Bootstrap...).

I’m extremely passionate about web development and design in all forms and I love helping people build and improve their online presence. Aside from web development, I enjoy arts, literature, photography and music, to name a few.
